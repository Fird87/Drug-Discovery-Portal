About Molplex

Molplex is  a new class of pharmaceutical company using highly automated decision making systems to identify  new treatments for infection, cancer and other serious diseases. Our pipeline of small-molecule therapeutics is driven by our proprietary platform for drug optimisation, Optiplex™. A highly scalable system combined with the latest in laboratory automation, delivering safer and more effective drugs. We discover novel treatments quicker and cost effectively, aiming to improve the health of patients around the globe. Molplex Pharmaceuticals is built on the following fundamentals:

  • Delivering value to shareholders by licensing  a pipeline of small molecule pharmaceuticals for infectious diseases, dermatology, and oncology.
  • Concentrating Talent & Technology.  Good management, experienced scientists  and an integrated technology.
  • Engaging new projects by applying our highly scalable platform to develop safer and more effective drugs.
  • Achieving highest success rates, quickly and cost-effectively.

Organisation

Founded in 2010, Molplex is a privately-held company, backed by a mix of private capital VC's and government grants. By building and advancing a valuable pipeline of novel compounds, Molplex continues to attract partners and investors worldwide.
